Bio


I am a Masters Student at USC, majoring in Computer Science, where I am working with Professor Xuezhe Ma and PhD student MingChang Chiu .

Before coming to USC, I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science at New York University, where I worked with Professor Mutiara Sondjaja.

I am generally interested in applied Machine Learning, but my recent interests have involved mostly in Computer Vision, but I am open to learning more about Computer Graphics.

I am interested in a variety of different fields, including the fields above and, but not limited to, Natural Language Processing and Game Development.
