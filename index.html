Bio


I am a Masters Student at USC, majoring in Computer Science, where I am working with Professor Xuezhe Ma.

Before coming to USC, I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science at New York University, where I worked with Professor Mutiara Sondjaja. She was really nice to me.

I am generally interested in applied Machine Learning, but my recent interests have involved Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. I am interested in a variety of different fields.
